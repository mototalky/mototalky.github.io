Moto Talky

What is Moto Talky ?

This is to enable the communication between vehicles to socialize our thoughts,emotions and save us from critical situations. Also, it would enable us to help those who are in need. It will remind us to take care of our vehicles on timely basis and have their valid documents all the time where ever you go !! It is a companion App to guide your need on the road like ATM, Petrol Bunk, Hotels, Service centers etc.
